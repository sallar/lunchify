Hi. This is Lunchify.

An app to help you eat wisely in a workday! Created by Kiosked Hackers
in Kiosked Hackaton № 1

We have the most complete
database of restaurants
and their lunch menues.

Nearby Places

Lunchify detects your location and shows you the best lunch places nearby. You can also see all options in one place. Works on iOS and Android.

HipChat Bot

We have a robot sitting in your HipChat™ all the time, you can ask him questions and he will tell you what are the best options for lunch today.

The Lunchify Button™

We offer a big, red button in the office. You can press it anytime, and it will send you lunch options nearby AND their menues in HipChat™. We’re that smart.
